## 2026 Offseason Power Rankings

Six seasons in, the BMFFFL's talent distribution has never been sharper. Some teams are locked in as perennial contenders. Others are deep in rebuild mode. And a few are right in the murky middle — competitive but not champions.

Here's where every team stands entering the 2026 offseason.

---

## The Rankings

### 1. MLSchools12 — Schoolcraft Football Team
**2025 Record**: 13-1 | **Career**: 68-15 (.819) | **Championships**: 2 (2021, 2024)

There is no argument for anyone else at #1. MLSchools12 has the best all-time record in BMFFFL history, two championships, and has made the playoffs in every single season. Their 2026 roster is anchored by **Lamar Jackson** (QB1), **CeeDee Lamb** (WR1), **Christian McCaffrey** (RB1, age 29), **Josh Jacobs** (RB2, age 28), and **Jaylen Waddle** (WR2, age 27).

The concern: McCaffrey and Jacobs are entering the declining phase of their dynasty careers. And the TE room (Hunter Henry, 31; George Kittle, 32) is a liability. Two consecutive semifinal exits (2023, 2025) as the 1-seed mean there's playoff variance to acknowledge.

**Outlook**: Win-now window is 1-2 years. The dynasty core needs a reboot after the McCaffrey/Jacobs era ends.

**2026 Dynasty Grade: A**

### 2. Tubes94 — Whale Tails
**2025 Record**: 10-4 | **Career**: 34-36 (.486) | **Championships**: 0 | **Runner-up**: 2025

Tubes94 made a run to the 2025 championship game and came up short. But what's more important is the roster underneath: **Bijan Robinson** (RB, age 24) and **Breece Hall** (RB, age 24) are two of the best young running backs in the BMFFFL. **Trevor Lawrence** (QB, age 26) is a rising dynasty Superflex asset.

The issue: WR room is thin. Tubes94 needs a WR infusion — either via the 2026 draft or a trade.

**Outlook**: With Robinson, Hall, and Lawrence as the core, Tubes94 has 4-5 years of contention window. This is a team on the ascent.

**2026 Dynasty Grade: A-**

## Sleeper Platform Guide for BMFFFL Owners

The BMFFFL moved to Sleeper in 2020 and has been there ever since. Sleeper is built specifically for dynasty leagues — it handles long-term roster management, taxi squads, waiver wire, and trading better than any other platform.

This guide covers the features BMFFFL owners use most.

---

## Getting Started

**Download the Sleeper app** on iOS or Android, or use the web app at sleeper.com. The BMFFFL league ID is `1312123497203376128` — search for it in the app or follow the invite link your commissioner shared.

Your username is your BMFFFL identifier: MLSchools12, Cogdeill11, Tubes94, etc. These are permanent — once set, they don't change.

## Roster Management

### Active Roster vs. Taxi Squad
Your active roster (15 slots) is what you start from each week. Your taxi squad (3 slots) is a separate development pipeline — those players don't take active roster spots and can't be started until you call them up.

**How to move a player to taxi**: In the roster view, tap the player and select "Move to Taxi." Only eligible players (first or second NFL season) can be taxied.

**Calling up a taxi player**: Before the season starts, decide which taxi players are ready to contribute. You'll need to cut an active roster player to make room.

### The Injured Reserve Slot
Players on the NFL's official IR or PUP lists are eligible for your Sleeper IR slot. Move injured players there immediately — it frees up an active roster spot. To activate a player from IR, you must cut someone to make room.

## Waiver Wire and FAAB

### How FAAB Works
BMFFFL uses **Free Agent Acquisition Budget** (FAAB) bidding. Each team gets $10,000 per season. Waivers process Tuesday nights.

To claim a player:
1. Go to the "Players" tab
2. Find the free agent you want
3. Tap "Add" and enter your bid
4. All bids are blind — you don't see what others bid
5. Highest bid wins; ties broken by waiver priority

**Strategy tip**: Most FAAB claims are worth $0 (backup RBs, thin depth). Save your budget for legitimate breakouts and injury replacements. In a dynasty league, you often don't need to spend much on waivers — your young players on taxi are more valuable than most free agents.

### Free Agency During the Offseason
After the season ends, free agency opens for direct pickups (no bidding). FAAB resets to $10,000 when the new season begins.

## Trading

Sleeper makes trading straightforward:

1. Go to the player you want to trade for
2. Tap "Propose Trade"
3. Select what you're offering (players, picks, or both)
4. Send to the other owner

### Trading Draft Picks
Future draft picks are tradeable in Sleeper. When offering picks, you'll see your current pick inventory — picks you own from trades or your own draft slots.

**Pick notation**: "1.01" = first pick in round 1. "3.07" = 7th pick in round 3. When a pick is traded, the new owner holds it and drafts with it when the time comes.

### Trade Review Period
After both owners agree, the BMFFFL has no commissioner veto period (unless the commissioner manually blocks it). Trades process immediately once accepted.

## Draft Day

### Rookie Draft Setup
The BMFFFL rookie draft is the first Friday of June each year. It's a **4-round linear draft** on Sleeper.

**Queuing picks**: Before the draft, queue up your preferred players in order. If you're on the clock and don't pick in time, Sleeper will auto-draft from your queue.

**Draft timer**: The BMFFFL uses a standard pick timer (check settings before the draft). Don't let the clock run out.

### Startup Draft (Historical)
The 2020 startup draft is how most of the current BMFFFL roster was built. Startup drafts use auction or snake format — ours used snake in 2020.

## Matchups and Scoring

### Setting Your Lineup
Lineups lock when a player's game starts. Set your lineup before Thursday night (for Thursday games) and before the 1pm Sunday slate.

**Automated lineup**: Sleeper can auto-set your lineup. Don't rely on this for important weeks — review your choices manually, especially for injuries.

### Viewing Scores
During game days, the Live Scoring view in Sleeper shows projected scores, real-time points, and position-by-position breakdowns. The app is generally reliable for live scoring.

### Points Per Game vs. Cumulative
Dynasty decisions should be based on points per game, not cumulative seasonal totals. A player who missed 4 games due to injury isn't necessarily worse than their total suggests.

## League Chat and Notifications

Sleeper has a built-in chat for each league. BMFFFL owners use this for trash talk, trade discussions, and breaking news.

**Enable push notifications** for:
- Trade offers (so you don't miss a deal)
- Waiver results
- Lineup lock reminders

Turn off notifications for weekly opponent scores if you find them distracting.

## The Sleeper API

For the more technically minded, Sleeper offers a public API at `https://api.sleeper.app/v1`. The BMFFFL website uses this API to power the analytics tools.

Key endpoints:
- `GET /league/{league_id}` — league info
- `GET /league/{league_id}/rosters` — all 12 team rosters
- `GET /league/{league_id}/users` — owner info
- `GET /league/{league_id}/matchups/{week}` — weekly scores

The API is read-only and requires no authentication for public league data. BMFFFL league ID: `1312123497203376128`.

## Common Issues

**Player not showing in my roster**: Verify the player isn't on your taxi squad — they appear separately.

**Can't pick up a free agent**: Check your FAAB balance and ensure the player is actually available (not on another roster's taxi).

**Trade offer stuck in pending**: The other owner needs to accept or decline. Reminder: trades don't process until both sides agree.

**Wrong player scored in my lineup**: Sleeper sometimes has data delays. Check back after the game ends — final scoring usually updates within 30 minutes of game completion.

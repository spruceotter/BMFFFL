# 2018 Season: The One MLSchools12 Lost

*MLSchools12 went 11-2, led the league in scoring, reached the championship game — and lost. The only time in ESPN-era BMFFFL history that the Murder Boners made the final and didn't win it.*

---

## The Season at a Glance

The 2018 season was, in one sense, a clean story: the best team in the league got to the championship and lost to a team that arguably deserved to be there. SexMachineAndyD (Stand Against Trade Rape — Mike Vieyra) went 9-4, third in the regular season, and won by 32 points in the final. It wasn't a fluke. They outscored the Murder Boners 138 to 106 on championship Sunday.

But context matters: three teams finished 9-4 — Cogdeill11 (1,941.0 pts), SexMachineAndyD (1,899.1 pts), and mmoodie12 (1,657.9 pts). The difference between a 3rd seed and a 4th seed was points tiebreaker. The playoff bracket shaped the outcome as much as anything.

**MLSchools12 went 11-2 again and didn't win.** That's two straight years of 11-2 or better regular-season records ending without a ring. The pattern was becoming a defining feature of the league.

## Champion: SexMachineAndyD (Stand Against Trade Rape — Mike Vieyra)

**Record**: 9-4 (3rd seed) | **Championship score**: 138.66

Mike Vieyra's Stand Against Trade Rape was the second straight champion in BMFFFL history to win from outside the top two seeds. They went 9-4 in the regular season — good enough for third — then beat a 6th seed, a 2nd seed, and the 1st seed in consecutive weeks.

The 138-point championship performance was commanding. This wasn't a low-scoring grind like 2017's 109-point final — SexMachineAndyD scored and scored and beat the best team in the league cleanly.

Mike Vieyra would continue in the league under this account before moving to the Sleeper era. The 2018 title remains his.

## Key Narratives

**The only championship MLSchools12 ever lost (in the final).**
MLSchools12 has been to multiple championship games across BMFFFL history. 2018 is the one they lost. 11-2 regular season, top scorer in the league at 1,956.7 points — and they couldn't win when it counted. The loss adds texture to the dynasty narrative: dominance without perfection.

**Three teams at 9-4, one wins it all.**
The 2018 regular season ended with a three-way tie at 9-4. Tiebreakers separated seeds 2, 3, and 4. The team that ended up 4th (mmoodie12) didn't even come close in the playoffs. The team that ended up 3rd won the championship. Seeding is fragile.

**The bottom dropped out.**
JuicyBussy and Cmaleski both went 2-11. Dan_gaudy went 3-10. The bottom third of the 2018 league was dramatically weaker than the top third. The league was stratifying.

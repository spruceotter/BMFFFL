# 2020 Season: The Founding Year

*BMFFFL's first full season on Sleeper ended in a four-point championship — and introduced every pattern that would define the next five years.*

---

## The Season at a Glance

The 2020 NFL season was unlike any before it: no preseason, COVID protocols in every stadium, condensed timelines and chaotic injury reports. Dynasty fantasy went with it — player availability was unpredictable, and the teams that managed depth survived. BMFFFL launched on Sleeper this year, establishing the records that define our history. Everything before this lives on ESPN. Everything after starts here.

**The Murder Boners (MLSchools12) ran the table.** 11-2 record, 2,115 points — the best team in the league by every measure. They would be eliminated in the semifinals. This was not the last time.

## Key Narratives

**Best record doesn't win the ring.**
MLSchools12 went 11-2 and scored 2,115 points. They lost in the semis. This is the central BMFFFL story. It started here and has repeated four more times since.

**The Cinderella arc**
eldridsm went 8-5, drew the 4th seed, beat the 5th in round 1, torched the #1 seed for 181 in the semis, and fell 4.76 points short of a championship. If you're in the playoffs, you have a shot.

**The COVID QB shuffle**
With disrupted practice schedules and unpredictable performance, having veteran QB stability mattered more than upside. Cogdeill11's Watson-Tannehill pairing held together through the chaos.

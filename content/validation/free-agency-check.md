## Key Notes for Dynasty Content

### Kenneth Walker III (#3)
The claimed "$45M" is the **max value** of the deal. The base contract value is $43.05M (3yr, AAV $14.35M). The guaranteed money ($28.7M) is accurate. Use "3yr/$43.05M (max $45M)" for precision.

### DJ Moore Trade (#6)
The trade structure was: **Bears send Moore + their own 2026 5th-round pick (No. 165) to Bills; Bills send their 2026 2nd-round pick (No. 60) to Bears.** The claim that "Bears received a 2nd round pick" is directionally correct but omits that Chicago also gave up a 5th-rounder.

### Geno Smith (#12)
This was a **trade**, not a free agent signing. Smith came from the **Las Vegas Raiders**, not via open market. The Jets paid a 6th-round pick. Factually, "Geno Smith → New York Jets" is correct as a destination, but the mechanism differs from a standard free agency signing.

### Kyle Pitts Franchise Tag (#10)
The claimed tag value of $14.3M was the **RB tag** (Breece Hall's number). The TE franchise tag is ~$16.3M. The claim the Falcons tagged Pitts is correct; the dollar figure cited appears to be confused with Hall's tag.

**Purpose:** Validate player statistics cited in dynasty fantasy football website articles

## Verification Status Key

- **CORRECT** — stat matches available data (within ~5%)
- **APPROXIMATE** — stat is close but not exact (within ~10%)
- **INCORRECT** — stat is significantly wrong; actual figure noted
- **UNVERIFIABLE** — no reliable data found

## Summary of Findings

**Correct / Approximate:** 0 fully correct, 1 approximate (Loveland — directionally right, numbers slightly off)

**Incorrect:** 11 of 12 claims contain significant errors

### Key Issues Identified

1. **Shedeur Sanders** — The website article appears to have used projected or fabricated stats. Sanders was a 5th-round pick who barely played; the claimed line describes a borderline-elite rookie QB season.

2. **Omarion Hampton** — Stats reflect a full healthy season; Hampton only played 9 games before a hamstring injury ended his year.

3. **Ashton Jeanty** — Rushing yards and YPC are inflated; team situation (poor Raiders OL) hurt his production significantly vs. college expectations.

4. **Tetairoa McMillan** — Stats are close but the article sets thresholds (85+, 1,100+) that McMillan didn't quite clear. He did win OROY.

5. **Elic Ayomanor** — Drastically overstated; Ayomanor was a rotational piece, not a featured receiver.

6. **Anthony Richardson** — Wrong injury (shoulder vs. orbital fracture) and severe understatement of absence (8 games vs. essentially entire season, not a starter).

7. **Jonathan Taylor** — One of the most egregious errors; Taylor had a legitimate MVP-caliber season that the article drastically undersells.

8. **Javonte Williams** — Wrong team entirely (article says DEN; Williams played for DAL). Stats are also off by a wide margin in the wrong direction.

9. **Zay Flowers** — Career year understated significantly.

10. **Sam LaPorta** — Numbers are somewhat close on a per-game basis but LaPorta only played 9 games; citing a full-season projection as actuals is misleading. The 2024 baseline cited (86/889) actually matches LaPorta's 2023 rookie season numbers.

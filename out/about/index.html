About BMFFFL

The Best MFing Fantasy Football League — a 12-team dynasty league built for the obsessed. Founded in 2020, we've traded picks, celebrated upsets, and crowned five unique champions across six seasons.

Draft & Season Calendar

Rookie Draft

First Friday of June

4 rounds, linear order (no snake). Draft order determined by reverse final standings. Picks are tradeable.

Regular Season

NFL Weeks 1–14

13 regular-season weeks. Each team plays every other team once, with select rematches. Top 6 teams make the playoffs.

Trade Deadline

End of Week 14

No trades after the regular season ends. All trades must be completed before Week 14 locks.

Playoffs

NFL Weeks 15–17

Top 6 seeds qualify. Byes for top 2 seeds in the first round. Single-elimination semifinals and finals.

Waivers & FAAB

FAAB Budget

$10,000

Per season, does not carry over

Waiver System

  • Blind bidding — all bids submitted simultaneously
  • Minimum bid: $0 (free pickup on no-bid players)
  • Waivers process Wednesday nights

Season Checklist

Commissioner responsibilities across the full league year

🌱

Pre-Season

  • □Set draft date and confirm all managers are available
  • □Confirm rosters and enforce keeper deadlines
  • □Set bye week rules and confirm tiebreaker procedures
  • □Reset FAAB budgets for all teams
⚡

In-Season

  • □Process waivers every week — confirm FAAB bids run on schedule
  • □Monitor and communicate injury reports as needed
  • □Handle trade protests and disputes within 48 hours
🏁

Post-Season

  • □Crown the champion — announce the final score and results
  • □Run the annual awards vote with the league
  • □Set next year's draft order based on standings
  • □Collect dues and confirm all managers returning

Commissioner History

Six seasons of governance under Grandes

20

2020

Grandes founds BMFFFL. First draft held. cogdeill11 wins inaugural championship.

21

2021

Taxi squad added by league vote (10-2). Grandes establishes formal dispute process.

22

2022

Keeper limit expanded to 3. Grandes wins championship — commissioner takes the crown.

23

2023

FAAB implemented by near-unanimous vote (11-1). Replaces waiver wire priority system.

24

2024

Playoff expansion to 6 teams fails in vote (5-7). Grandes rules stand firm.

25

2025

Six seasons complete. League remains 12 managers, fully intact. Governance holds.

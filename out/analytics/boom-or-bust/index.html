Profile Classification Guide

EXPLOSIVE BOOM

High variance + dangerous avg. Most lethal opponent on the right week. Hard to game-plan for.

HIGH FLOOR BOOM

Dominant average with boom upside. Can be caught on an off week but rarely underperforms badly.

ELITE CONSISTENT

The gold standard. High avg, low variance. Rarely loses because of their own score.

BOOM-CAPABLE

Mid avg but high variance. Dangerous in single-elimination playoffs — streaks are their weapon.

CONSISTENT

Predictable and reliable. You know what you're getting. Good, not great, every week.

VOLATILE BUST

Low avg + high variance. Wild swings that usually end up disappointing. Hard to trust in any format.

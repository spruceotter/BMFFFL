The Dynasty: MLSchools12

MLSchools12 is the most decorated franchise in BMFFFL history — four all-time championships spanning the ESPN era (2016, 2019) and Sleeper era (2021, 2024). Across the Sleeper era the Schoolcraft Football Team has never posted a losing regular-season record, holds the all-time best career win percentage at .819 (68-15), and made the playoffs every single year. The 2025 regular season record of 13-1 — the best single-season record in league history — set up a playoff run that fell just short of a fifth ring.

Greatest Cinderella Run: JuicyBussy 2023

No championship in BMFFFL history is more celebrated than JuicyBussy's 2023 run as the #6 seed. A 7-7 regular season record barely squeaked into the field — and then CMC, Justin Jefferson, Tyreek Hill, and Patrick Mahomes went on a tear. The #2, #3, and #4 seeds all fell in sequence. It remains the only time a 6-seed has won a championship in league history, and it probably won't happen again.

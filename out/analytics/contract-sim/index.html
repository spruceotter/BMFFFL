Future Feature — Design Preview

This page is a speculative design, not a live system. No contracts are in effect. This preview exists for community discussion about whether BMFFFL should adopt a salary cap format in a future season.

Contract System Design

What would BMFFFL look like with salary caps, player contracts, and dead money? A detailed design proposal for community consideration.

Proposed System Rules

Salary Cap

$200 per team per season. Cannot be exceeded. All players must have a contract to be on your roster.

Contract Lengths

Players signed to 1, 2, or 3-year deals. After expiration, players enter free agency.

Rookie Contracts

Years 1–2: $5–$15 based on dynasty draft slot. 1.01 pick = $15, 1.12 pick = $5. Controlled cost for young players.

Free Agency

Open market bidding similar to FAAB. Managers submit blind bids. Highest bid wins — salary set by bid amount.

Dead Cap

Cutting a player under contract leaves dead money equal to remaining contract value. Dead cap counts against your $200 limit.

Trade Salary Matching

Traded players bring their contracts. Trades must be within 25% salary match of each other, or both sides agree to absorb the difference.

Narrative Arcs

Five dynasties defined not by a single season, but by what happened across all of them.

The Dynasty Peak

MLSchools12

MLSchools12 went 13-1 in 2023 — the single-season record — yet JuicyBussy won the championship that year as the 6-seed. MLSchools12 came back in 2024 to win their 4th all-time championship from the 3-seed. The most decorated franchise in BMFFFL history, now with rings in 2016, 2019, 2021, and 2024.

The Great Collapse

Cogdeill11

The founding champion. 10-3 in 2020 with a title. Then 8-5, 6-7, 5-9, 5-9, 4-10. A 6-win decline over 5 years — the steepest and most sustained downfall in league history.

The Resurrection

tdtd19844

3-11 in 2022. Dead last. Then 6-8. Then 8-6. Then the 2025 championship. tdtd19844 rebuilt from the bottom and claimed a ring — the greatest comeback arc this league has ever seen.

The Commissioner's Fall

Grandes

Grandes won it all in 2022. In 2025, they finished 5-9 and were relegated to the Moodie Bowl. The arc from champion to consolation bracket in three seasons is a cautionary tale in dynasty roster management.

The Newcomer's Rise

Tubes94

Tubes94 entered the league in 2021 at 7-6 — respectable for a first year. By 2024 and 2025 they'd reached 11-3. Five seasons, 4 more wins, no signs of slowing. The best onboarding trajectory in BMFFFL history.

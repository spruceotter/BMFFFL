Power Rankings Automation

Season 7 Preview — Stat-based weekly power rankings system

Manual mode — data updated at season start

How It Works

Four components are normalized 0–100 for each team, then blended using the weights below. Final Power Score = weighted average, scaled to 0–100.

Wins

40%

Win/loss record normalized across all 12 teams. Winning is the primary signal — schedule-adjusted.

Points For

30%

Total points scored season-to-date, normalized. Raw scoring output cuts through lucky wins and unlucky losses.

Recent Form

20%

Last 3 weeks W/L/PPG weighted equally. Hot streaks matter — a team catching fire gets rewarded.

Strength of Schedule

10%

Average opponent PPG faced. Winning against strong opponents inflates your score; padding against weak ones is discounted.

The Formula

Power Score = (Wins × 0.40) + (Points For × 0.30) + (Recent Form × 0.20) + (SOS × 0.10)

Each component is normalized 0–100 before weighting. Raw values are pulled from Sleeper each Monday morning during the season.

Automation Design

What the auto-generator would look like in production during the season.

Weekly Pipeline

  1. 01

    Pull Sleeper Scores

    Every Monday 12:00 PM — fetch final scores for all matchups via Sleeper API.

  2. 02

    Recalculate Components

    Normalize wins, points for, last-3-weeks form, and SOS faced for all 12 teams.

  3. 03

    Compute Power Scores

    Apply the weighted formula. Flag any movement of 3+ spots from prior week.

  4. 04

    Generate Insights

    Auto-produce Bimfle's Commentary for major movers, streaks, and collapse alerts.

  5. 05

    Publish to Page

    Update this page with new rankings, movement indicators, and insight callouts.

Roster Value Integration

Monte Carlo simulation to project playoff probability from current power ranking and remaining schedule.

FAAB Strategy Guide

Six principles that separate the managers who spend smart from the ones who blow $40 on a bye-week handcuff.

1

The 70% Rule

Never spend more than 70% of your budget before Week 10. The best values emerge mid-season.

2

Injury Windows

When a star player gets injured, bid aggressively in Week 1. Replacement value spikes then falls.

3

Handcuff Value

Handcuffs for your own studs are worth $5–15. Never bid $0 on your RB1's backup.

4

The Zero Bid

Free agents who clear waivers (no one bid) can be added for free after Tuesday 4 AM. Patience often beats spending.

5

Late-Season FAAB

Playoff-seeded managers should hoard FAAB for the final push. Eliminated teams can afford to overspend.

6

The Decoy Bid

Submit a $1 bid on low-value players to use up opponents' budget mental bandwidth. Psychological warfare.

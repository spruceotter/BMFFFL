BMFFFL 2026 Offseason Outlook: Every Team's Situation

FlintMarch 15, 20269 min read

BMFFFL 2026 Offseason Outlook

The 2025 season is over. tdtd19844 is the champion. MLSchools12 went 13-1 and lost in the semis. Again. The rookie draft is eleven weeks away. Here's where every team stands.

🏆 tdtd19844 — THE Shameful Saggy sack

2025 result: 8-6, 4th seed → Champion Draft capital: 7 picks (no 2026 1st — traded to eldridm20 for DK Metcalf) Phase: Defend + reload

The most improbable playoff run in BMFFFL history. Upset the 13-1 MLSchools12 in the semis, then beat Tubes94 in the final. The regular season 8-6 record understated their late-season scoring peak.

The move that defined the season: Acquiring DK Metcalf from eldridm20 at Week 10 for a 2026 1st-round pick. That pick will land around 1.12 (champion's slot). A late first for the final piece of a championship run is exactly the trade you make.

2026 situation: No dynasty has ever defended a BMFFFL championship. The pattern is clear: champions regress, field catches up. Without a 2026 1st, roster replenishment is harder. But Caleb Williams (24) as QB1 could be on the verge of a real breakout with Chicago in Year 3. The window is still open.

🥈 Tubes94 — Whale Tails

2025 result: 10-4, 2nd seed → Runner-up Draft capital: 9 picks (own late 1st [1.11], Grandes' 2026 2nd [2.01]) Phase: Contend — now

The most remarkable rebuild arc in the league: "Burn it all" (4-10) in 2022 → back-to-back runner-up finishes in 2024 and 2025. The roster is legitimately championship-caliber. What's missing is closing.

Key asset: The Grandes 2026 2nd (pick 2.01 — first pick of the second round). If the 2026 class has real WR/RB value in the second round, this is premium capital.

The question: Can they convert a runner-up finish into a ring in 2026? The third consecutive year of playoff depth is either dynasty consistency building or the ceiling of this roster.

MLSchools12 — Schoolcraft Football Team

2025 result: 13-1, 1st seed → Lost semis to tdtd19844 Draft capital: 9 picks (own late 1st, JuicyBussy 2026 2nd, JuicyBussy 2027 1st) Phase: Win-now (perpetual)

The pattern that defines this league: 13-1 in 2023, lost semis. 13-1 in 2025, lost semis. The regular-season dominance is proven — .820 all-time win rate, 100% playoff rate, two championships. But the #1 seed curse is also proven: 1-3 in the semis from that position.

The core question for 2026: Floor-based construction (Lamar Jackson, CeeDee Lamb, CMC) dominates a 14-week regular season but struggles in single-game elimination. Add ceiling — a boom-or-bust WR who can drop 35 in a good week.

JuicyBussy's 2027 1st is the most interesting future asset in the league. If JuicyBussy has a bad season (zero draft picks until 2027), that pick could be a 1.01 or 1.02. It's MLSchools12's hedge for the future.

eldridm20 — Franks Little Beauties

2025 result: 6-8, missed playoffs Draft capital: 9 picks, 2 2026 firsts (own + tdtd19844's) Phase: Surprise contender?

The sleeper of this offseason. Holding two 2026 first-round picks — their own (mid-to-late, 6-8 team) and tdtd19844's (1.12 range) — eldridm20 can either make a big trade-up for a specific player or simply build for the future. 2023 was their career peak (runner-up); 2025 was regression. Two firsts gives them flexibility.

Grandes — El Rioux Grandes

2025 result: 4-10, Moodie Bowl Draft capital: 16 picks (3 2026 1sts, including likely 1.01) Phase: Full nuclear rebuild

From 2022 champion to 2025 last place — the dynasty arc in its most complete form. But Grandes has positioned themselves perfectly for what comes next: 3 first-round picks in 2026, 16 total across two years. This is the most aggressive rebuild in league history.

The bet: If the 2026 NFL Draft class produces 3+ legitimate dynasty difference-makers in the first round of BMFFFL's draft, Grandes could assemble a championship-caliber roster within 2 years. If the class is shallow, those picks are still good value over time.

Hold: Xavier Worthy (22) — the only piece worth keeping while the rebuild runs.

JuicyBussy — Juicy Bussy

2025 result: 7-7, 5th seed, lost wild card Draft capital: 0 picks through 2027 Round 2 Phase: Win-now (only option)

Zero picks until 2027 Round 3 means every offseason move must be player-for-player. The WR room is historically deep — AJ Brown, Tee Higgins, Amon-Ra St. Brown, Nico Collins — but the roster has one point of catastrophic failure: Joe Burrow. If he gets injured, the season ends. Trading WR surplus for a QB2 with dynasty upside is the only sensible insurance move.

The fire sale that got them here funded Grandes' entire rebuild. Whatever JuicyBussy got in return had better pay off in 2026-2027, because they have zero draft flexibility otherwise.

SexMachineAndyD

2025 result: 9-5, 3rd seed, lost wild card Draft capital: 5 picks, thin Phase: Hold / marginal buyer

2024 runner-up, 2025 first-round exit. The pick capital is thin (traded a 2026 1st to Grandes). Patience is right here — a middle-tier offseason.

rbr — Really Big Rings

2025 result: 5-9, missed playoffs Draft capital: 8 picks, all own Phase: Sell veterans / buy core

Two-time runner-up (2021, 2022), zero championships. Derrick Henry (32), Travis Kelce (36) — veteran talent that's dynasty-dead. Justin Jefferson (26) and Jayden Daniels (25) are the foundation to build around. The right move is trading the aging veterans to contenders for picks and building around Jefferson.

Cogdeill11

2025 result: 5-9, missed playoffs Draft capital: 8 picks, all own Phase: Rebuild

2020 champion. The arc is complete — from first champion to multi-year rebuild. QBs on IR, Kirk Cousins and Geno Smith active. The roster is built around Jahmyr Gibbs, Omarion Hampton, and Chris Olave (25). Trading Olave to a contender for a first-round pick is the right rebuild move — he's 25 and generates premium interest.

Cmaleski — Showtyme Boyz

2025 result: 6-8, 6th seed, lost semis Draft capital: 7 picks (no own 2026 1st) Phase: Bridge year

Scored 1,990 points in 2025 — 2nd in the league — with a 6-8 record. The scoring ability is real. Drake London at 25 and Anthony Richardson at 24 are the dynasty anchors. Holds SexMachineAndyD's 2026 2nd and 4th as picks. Not a seller, not a buyer — wait and see what the NFL Draft brings.

eldridsm

2025 result: 5-9, missed playoffs Draft capital: 8 picks, holds MLSchools12's 2027 1st Phase: Sleeper rebuild

The sleeper asset: MLSchools12's 2027 1st. If MLSchools12 has a bad 2026 season (unlikely, but possible), that pick could be a premium asset. Otherwise eldridsm is building toward a 2027 window with their 2026 draft selections.

Escuelas — Booty Cheeks

2025 result: 6-8, missed playoffs Draft capital: 8 own picks Phase: Year 5 rebuild, showing signs of life

First 6-win season since joining in 2022. The young roster is developing. The 2026 draft picks are more valuable than any trade target — hold them and let the build continue.

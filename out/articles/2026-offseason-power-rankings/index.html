2026 Offseason Power Rankings: Who Has the Best Roster Right Now?

FlintMarch 16, 202613 min read
Data valid as of Invalid Date. Stats may have changed since publication.

2026 Offseason Power Rankings

Six seasons in, the BMFFFL's talent distribution has never been sharper. Some teams are locked in as perennial contenders. Others are deep in rebuild mode. And a few are right in the murky middle — competitive but not champions.

Here's where every team stands entering the 2026 offseason.

The Rankings

1. MLSchools12 — Schoolcraft Football Team

2025 Record: 13-1 | Career: 68-15 (.819) | Championships: 2 (2021, 2024)

There is no argument for anyone else at #1. MLSchools12 has the best all-time record in BMFFFL history, two championships, and has made the playoffs in every single season. Their 2026 roster is anchored by Lamar Jackson (QB1), CeeDee Lamb (WR1), Christian McCaffrey (RB1, age 29), Josh Jacobs (RB2, age 28), and Jaylen Waddle (WR2, age 27).

The concern: McCaffrey and Jacobs are entering the declining phase of their dynasty careers. And the TE room (Hunter Henry, 31; George Kittle, 32) is a liability. Two consecutive semifinal exits (2023, 2025) as the 1-seed mean there's playoff variance to acknowledge.

Outlook: Win-now window is 1-2 years. The dynasty core needs a reboot after the McCaffrey/Jacobs era ends.

2026 Dynasty Grade: A

2. Tubes94 — Whale Tails

2025 Record: 10-4 | Career: 34-36 (.486) | Championships: 0 | Runner-up: 2025

Tubes94 made a run to the 2025 championship game and came up short. But what's more important is the roster underneath: Bijan Robinson (RB, age 24) and Breece Hall (RB, age 24) are two of the best young running backs in the BMFFFL. Trevor Lawrence (QB, age 26) is a rising dynasty Superflex asset.

The issue: WR room is thin. Tubes94 needs a WR infusion — either via the 2026 draft or a trade.

Outlook: With Robinson, Hall, and Lawrence as the core, Tubes94 has 4-5 years of contention window. This is a team on the ascent.

2026 Dynasty Grade: A-

3. tdtd19844 — THE Shameful Saggy sack

2025 Record: 8-6 (champion) | Career: 36-47 (.434) | Championships: 1 (2025)

The defending champion. Their 2025 championship was built around Bijan Robinson (before the trade?) and a supporting cast that peaked at the right moment. As the 4th seed, they're not a conventional dynasty powerhouse — but the roster has real building blocks.

The question for 2026: can they reload after winning? The 2025 championship came as they were ascending. If their core is entering its prime in 2026-2028, they could string together multiple contention windows.

Outlook: Needs to be assessed after the 2026 NFL Draft. Pick capital and roster decisions this offseason will define their next 3-year arc.

2026 Dynasty Grade: B+

4. JuicyBussy — Juicy Bussy

2025 Record: Unknown | Career: 46-37 (.554) | Championships: 1 (2023)

JuicyBussy's .554 career win rate and 5/6 playoff appearances make them one of the more consistent franchises in BMFFFL history. The 2023 championship as a 6th seed showed they can win when it matters.

Their current roster details aren't fully documented, but their career consistency suggests a well-managed team that avoids catastrophic decisions.

Outlook: Likely in contention. A quiet team that doesn't make noise until they win.

2026 Dynasty Grade: B+

5. SexMachineAndyD

2025 Record: Runner-up in 2024 (10-4), unknown in 2025 | Career: 50-33 (.602) | Championships: 0

The most puzzling team in BMFFFL — second-best career win percentage (.602), zero championships, one runner-up. In 2024, they went 10-4, won the regular season as the top seed, and lost the championship.

This is a team that wins games but doesn't win titles. The question is whether their roster construction is fundamentally championship-caliber or just playoff-caliber.

Outlook: Another final appearance is within reach if they stay healthy and get favorable playoff matchups.

2026 Dynasty Grade: B

6. Grandes — El Rioux Grandes

2025 Record: Moodie Bowl loser (4-10) | Career: 42-41 (.506) | Championships: 1 (2022)

A study in volatility: 2022 champion as a 4th seed, 2025 Moodie Bowl loser. The swing between best and worst in three years is remarkable. What happened to the 2022 championship core?

The 2025 Moodie Bowl last-place finish gives Grandes draft capital — they may pick early-middle in the 2026 rookie draft. If they use it wisely, a bounce-back is possible.

Outlook: Rebuilding but not hopeless. The Moodie Bowl pick is real value in a strong 2026 class.

2026 Dynasty Grade: C+

7. rbr — Really Big Rings

2025 Record: Unknown | Career: 44-39 (.530) | Championships: 0 | Runner-ups: 2 (2021, 2022)

Back-to-back runner-up finishes in 2021 and 2022 without a championship is the defining narrative. The team that always gets close but never wins.

The real question entering 2026: are they still competitive or has the window closed? Career .530 win rate and 4 playoff appearances in 6 seasons says they've been consistently good but not great.

Outlook: Middle tier. Need to evaluate whether their current core justifies a win-now push or whether a light rebuild makes more sense.

2026 Dynasty Grade: C+

8. eldridsm

2025 Record: Unknown | Career: 41-42 (.494) | Championships: 0 | Runner-up: 2020

One playoff appearance in the last 4 years (based on career trajectory). The 2020 runner-up finish — losing to Cogdeill11 by 4.76 points — was their high-water mark.

Outlook: Needs a reset. Career .494 win rate at this stage in dynasty suggests a team in the middle class trap.

2026 Dynasty Grade: C

9. eldridm20

2025 Record: Unknown | Career: 39-44 (.470) | Championships: 0 | Runner-up: 2023

Two playoff appearances in 6 seasons, one runner-up. Career record below .500. The 2023 run to the finals was the bright spot; before and after, consistency has been elusive.

Outlook: Below .500 team entering 2026. May benefit from the 2026 draft class if they pick in the top half.

2026 Dynasty Grade: C-

10. Cogdeill11

2025 Record: 4-9 | Career: 38-45 (.458) | Championships: 1 (2020)

The hardest franchise story in BMFFFL. Won the first championship (2020), then declined every single season after. Now holds a career record of 38-45 and missed the playoffs for five consecutive years.

The 2026 rookie draft is their best opportunity to change trajectory — they hold multiple picks including 1.04 (approximately). The 2026 class is strong enough that smart selections could accelerate a rebuild.

Outlook: Full rebuild. 2026 picks + patience = 2028 contention window at earliest.

2026 Dynasty Grade: D+

11. Cmaleski

2025 Record: Unknown (finished last, holds 1.01) | Career: 36-47 (.434) | Championships: 0

Holding the 1.01 pick in a year with Jeremiyah Love available. This is the single best asset in the 2026 BMFFFL offseason. What Cmaleski does with that pick — and with picks 2.01, 3.01, 4.01 in a linear draft — will define their next 4 years.

Outlook: The pick makes this team relevant. Don't waste the 1.01.

2026 Dynasty Grade: D+ (pre-draft) / could jump to B with smart draft

12. Escuelas

2025 Record: 6-8 | Career: 15-41 (.268) | Championships: 0

Four seasons in the BMFFFL with 0 playoff appearances, but the trajectory is improving: 4-10, 2-12, 3-11, 6-8. That 6-8 in 2025 is their best season and shows real development.

Picking 1.02 in 2026 with multiple top picks across all four rounds, Escuelas has the most raw draft capital of any non-Cmaleski team.

Outlook: The rebuild is working. Smart 2026 draft selections could have them at 8-6 in 2027 and in playoff contention by 2028.

2026 Dynasty Grade: D (improving)

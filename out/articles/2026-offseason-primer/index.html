BMFFFL 2026 Offseason Primer

It's March. The 2025 season ended in January. The 2026 rookie draft is June 5. Here's everything that matters between now and then.

The State of Play

2025 champion: tdtd19844 (THE Shameful Saggy sack), 8-6, defeated Tubes94 152-135 in the final. Regular season king: MLSchools12 went 13-1 and lost in the semis. Again. The second time in three years. Biggest offseason story: JuicyBussy sold every draft pick in 2026 to Grandes. Zero picks through 2027 Round 2. Rebuild team to watch: Grandes has 3 first-round picks and 16 total in 2026-2027. The most capital-rich rebuild in BMFFFL history.

What Happened to Each Team

Championship tier (proven contenders)

  • tdtd19844 (Champion): No 2026 1st pick. Dynasty window is now. Defending in BMFFFL is historically impossible.
  • Tubes94 (Runner-up): Back-to-back finals appearances. Late 1st + 2.01. They need to close.
  • MLSchools12 (3rd): 13-1 into a semifinal loss. Two championships despite three semifinal exits as the #1 seed.

Near-contender tier

  • JuicyBussy: Best WR room in the league. Zero 2026 picks. All-in on the current roster.
  • SexMachineAndyD: Strongest dynasty roster by value (Malik Nabers, Brock Bowers, Garrett Wilson). 2024 runner-up. 2025 was a first-round exit.
  • Cmaleski: Drake Maye + Drake London + Travis Hunter (IR). Ceiling is very high if Hunter returns.

Rebuilding tier

  • Grandes: Nuclear rebuild. 4-10, Moodie Bowl, 16 picks incoming. 2026 championship contender: no. 2028 championship candidate: possible.
  • Cogdeill11: 2020 champion in a multi-year decline. Jahmyr Gibbs and Omarion Hampton are the foundation.
  • Escuelas: Year 5 rebuild, first 6-win season. Young pieces developing.
  • rbr: Justin Jefferson is the only reason to be optimistic. Henry and Kelce are expiring assets.
  • eldridm20: Two 2026 firsts. Josh Allen + Ja'Marr Chase = elite starting pair. Everything else is thin.
  • eldridsm: Holds MLSchools12's 2027 1st. Patient rebuild.

Key Dates — The 2026 Offseason Timeline

Right Now (March 2026)

NFL Free Agency is open (March 12). Dynasty values are shifting daily as:

  • Elite free agents sign to new teams → target shares change
  • Depth pieces sign, resolving committee questions
  • Coach hires finalize, clarifying offensive schemes

What to do now: Map your current roster against who you expected to be your 2026 starters. Has any key player moved to a dramatically worse (or better) situation? Act while values are in flux — buy players whose new situation is an upgrade before the market adjusts.

April 2026: NFL Draft

Date: April 24-26 — the single most important event for dynasty.

Landing spots are the decisive variable for 2026 rookie picks. A WR going to a pass-heavy offense with a clear target role is worth 40-50% more dynasty value than the same WR going to a run-first team. The NFL Draft creates this information.

What to watch:

  1. Where does the consensus WR1 (Tetairoa McMillan) land?
  2. Which QB goes in the top 10 — and where?
  3. Do any RBs land in RB-friendly backfields (committee-free)?
  4. Is there an unexpected TE1 who slides due to QB situations?

May 2026: Owners Meeting

Timing: ~3 weeks before the rookie draft

Key decisions:

  • Final 2026 draft order locked
  • Rule changes (TE premium? Taxi expansion?)
  • FAAB budget confirmed for 2026 season

June 5, 2026: Rookie Draft — The Main Event

Location: TBD (video call) Format: 4 rounds, linear order, first Friday of June

This is the moment that reshapes the league. Grandes picks three times in round 1. JuicyBussy sits out. The 2026 rookie class's WR depth means every round offers legitimate value.

Key pick slots to watch: 1.01, 1.05, 1.10, 2.01

July 2026: BMFFFL Free Agency

FAAB resets. Free agent pool opens. Picks up players cut from NFL rosters following final 53-man deadline.

September 2026: Season Begins

Year 1 of the post-JuicyBussy-fire-sale era. Year 4 of Tubes94 contention. Year 1 of Grandes' rebuild paying off (or not). Year 3 of Caleb Williams. The 2026 NFL season starts with this table set.

What Every Owner Should Be Doing Right Now

If you're a contender (tdtd19844, Tubes94, MLSchools12, JuicyBussy):

  • Map your 2026 starting lineup against free agency moves. Did any of your players just lose or gain a target share advantage?
  • Identify your one-game-ceiling problem. Contenders don't lose to bad teams in the playoffs — they lose to hot teams in single elimination. What's your variance play?

If you're mid-tier (SexMachineAndyD, Cmaleski, rbr, eldridm20):

  • SexMachineAndyD: The roster is already the best in the league. You need a championship-game-level season. Execute.
  • Cmaleski: Hold Travis Hunter. He changes the tier of this roster if healthy.
  • rbr: Sell Henry and Kelce now. The picks they return are more valuable than 2 weeks of production.
  • eldridm20: Two firsts + Allen/Chase. Stay patient. Don't trade Allen for anything.

If you're rebuilding (Grandes, Cogdeill11, Escuelas, eldridsm):

  • Grandes: Trust the process. Don't trade the picks. Be disciplined about selecting players with early career trajectory rather than established veterans.
  • Cogdeill11: Chris Olave is the most tradeable asset. Sell him to a contender for the first-round pick you need.
  • Escuelas: Hold every pick. Year 5 is too early to rush.
  • eldridsm: Hold MLSchools12's 2027 1st. Don't sell it until you know how MLSchools12's 2026 season goes.

The Superflex Impact

SUPER_FLEX means elite QBs are the most valuable dynasty asset in the league. In standard leagues, a QB scores in one slot. In BMFFFL, a QB can fill either the QB slot or the SUPER_FLEX slot — effectively letting teams play two QBs every week.

Dynasty implication: An elite QB (Lamar Jackson, Josh Allen, Jalen Hurts) is worth significantly more than an elite RB or WR, because they score in a slot that only QBs can efficiently fill. This is why the Sleeper era of BMFFFL is dominated by teams with two starting-caliber QBs.

Draft Rules

Annual Rookie Draft

Draft order for 2026: Reverse 2025 final standings, with tiebreaker by points scored. Champion picks last (1.12), last-place picks first (1.01).

Linear format: Unlike snake drafts, linear means the team picking 1.01 also picks 2.01, 3.01, and 4.01. This significantly advantages the last-place team — they pick first in every round, not just odd rounds.

Taxi Squad Rules

  • 5-player taxi squad for developmental players
  • Restricted to players within their first 3 years (Sleeper API confirmed: 3-year eligibility)
  • Players on taxi cannot be in the starting lineup
  • Taxi players can be promoted to the active roster at any time

Waiver Wire & Free Agency

FAAB strategy: 100% of your $10,000 budget is available from Week 1. Unspent FAAB does not carry over to the next season. The optimal strategy is to spend conservatively through the first half of the season (preserving budget for the stretch run) and bid aggressively on high-impact adds.

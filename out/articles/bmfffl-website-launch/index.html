The BMFFFL Website Is Live

Six seasons. Twelve teams. 257 trades. Five unique champions. One website.

The BMFFFL finally has a dedicated home on the internet — a place to track dynasty history, debate roster decisions, and document what has become one of the most competitive 12-team dynasty leagues around.

What's Here

League History

Six seasons of archives going back to 2020. Every champion, every runner-up, every Moodie Bowl loser. The full standings history, week by week, season by season. If it happened in the BMFFFL, it's documented.

Analytics Tools

The analytics section gives every BMFFFL manager data they can actually use:

  • Dynasty Rankings — 50 players ranked and filtered by position and owner
  • Roster Value Scores — How does your team stack up right now?
  • Owner Dashboard — Every manager's career stats and trends
  • Head-to-Head Matrix — All-time records between every pair of teams
  • Mock Draft Simulator — Practice your 2026 rookie draft
  • Trade Analyzer — Calculate trade value before you hit send
  • Landing Spot Tracker — Where are 2026 prospects going?
  • Free Agency Tracker — What the offseason moves mean for your roster

Articles

Everything from season previews and recaps to dynasty strategy and league history. Long reads, quick analysis, and evergreen guides for new and veteran managers alike.

What's Coming

The website will grow through the 2026 season with:

  • NFL Draft coverage (April 24-26) — landing spot analysis as picks happen
  • 2026 Rookie Draft hub — complete guide and pick-by-pick coverage in June
  • Weekly recaps during the regular season
  • Live Sleeper data integration — rosters, matchups, and standings pulling directly from the app
  • Trade history — all 257 trades documented

A Note on the Data

This site was built with accuracy as a priority. Every stat, every record, every owner career line has been cross-referenced against our internal data. Where we were uncertain, we flagged it.

The 2025 season data is pending Sleeper API final verification. All other seasons are confirmed.

If you spot an error — a wrong win count, an incorrect team name, a misattributed trade — email the commissioner or drop it in the league chat. We'll fix it.

To the 12 Managers

Whether you're MLSchools12 running the table or Escuelas finishing the rebuild — this site is for all of you. Six years of arguing in group chats, making deals at midnight, and watching Lamar Jackson go off while your bench watched.

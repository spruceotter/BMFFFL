BMFFFL Buyers & Sellers: 2026 Offseason Edition

Where does each of the 12 BMFFFL teams stand heading into the 2026 rookie draft? Who should be aggressive, who should be patient, and who needs to blow it up?

The Buyers

Buy aggressively — add established contributors, willing to give picks or depth.

MLSchools12 — BUY (Ceiling Assets)

The league's premier floor-without-ceiling roster. Two 13-1 seasons, two semifinal exits. Lamar Jackson, CeeDee Lamb, CMC, Jaylen Waddle — the consistency is there, but not the boom-or-bust upside that wins single-elimination playoff games.

The play: Buy a high-variance ascending WR who can drop 35 in a good week. Consider selling CMC — at 29 with a history of injuries, the trade window is closing. Use the return on a younger RB1 upgrade.

Buy: Ascending boom WR (target: Rome Odunze from Cogdeill11) Sell: Christian McCaffrey before the injury risk peaks

Tubes94 — BUY (Championship Now)

Back-to-back runner-up finishes. The championship window is open. The roster has a real TE problem — Kyle Pitts has underdelivered his entire career. Tubes94 should be aggressive buyers of an established TE or a second elite WR to push past the finish line. They have the Grandes 2026 2nd (2.01) as currency.

Buy: Established WR2/TE1 (targets: Chris Olave, Sam LaPorta) Hold: Bijan Robinson, Puka Nacua, Breece Hall — don't touch the foundation

tdtd19844 — BUY (Defend the Window)

Defending champion with no 2026 first-round pick. The WR room outside Brian Thomas and DK Metcalf is thin and aging. Caleb Williams (24) could be a real QB1 breakout in Year 3. The right buy is a complementary WR2 who can score behind the top two.

Buy: Young WR2 (targets: Rome Odunze or Drake London) Sell: Jonathan Taylor (sell-high coming off MVP-caliber season — 1,585 yards, 18 TDs — before age-28 cliff arrives in September 2026)

JuicyBussy — BUY (With Players Only)

Zero 2026 picks means any acquisition must be player-for-player. The WR room is elite — AJ Brown, Tee Higgins, Amon-Ra St. Brown, Nico Collins, George Pickens. But there's only one real QB (Joe Burrow), and if Burrow gets injured, the season is over. Trading WR surplus for a dynasty QB2 is the resilience play.

Buy: Dynasty QB2 via player trade (target: Caleb Williams from tdtd19844) Sell: George Pickens — fetch strong return while the WR market is crowded

The Sellers

Sell aggressively — prioritize picks and youth over win-now assets.

Grandes — SELL (Still Clearing)

16 picks coming in is extraordinary, but Grandes should continue moving any remaining veteran assets with near-term value. Patrick Mahomes (30) still holds meaningful trade value — sell him now before the age cliff becomes visible in trade negotiations. Mahomes could fetch a mid-first from a contending team that needs a QB upgrade.

Sell: Patrick Mahomes (to a contender for a 2027 first) Hold: Xavier Worthy (22, legitimate long-term dynasty asset)

rbr (Really Big Rings) — SELL (Aging Veterans)

Derrick Henry is 32. Travis Kelce is 36. Jared Goff is 31. Every one of these players has near-zero dynasty value. rbr should be moving Henry, Kelce, and Goff to win-now teams for pick returns, building assets around Justin Jefferson and Jayden Daniels.

Sell: Derrick Henry (before the cliff), Travis Kelce (immediately) Hold forever: Justin Jefferson

Cogdeill11 — SELL (Selective)

Kirk Cousins and Geno Smith as the active QB1/2. Three QBs on IR. This roster cannot compete in 2026 with the current quarterback situation. The right move is accepting the rebuild year and moving Chris Olave (25) to a contender for a first-round pick — he will generate premium interest.

Sell: Chris Olave to the highest bidder Hold: Jahmyr Gibbs, Omarion Hampton — these are the franchise foundation

The Holders

Hold — stay patient, neither aggressive buying nor selling.

SexMachineAndyD — HOLD

2024 runner-up. Limited pick capital (only 5 picks over 2 years). The core is good enough to compete without major moves. Watch the offseason before committing to any direction.

rbr — HOLD (after selling vets)

Once the veteran purge is complete (Henry, Kelce, Goff), the Justin Jefferson + Jayden Daniels core is worth holding and building around carefully.

eldridsm — HOLD (selective buy)

Holds MLSchools12's 2027 first — a sleeper asset. Patience is the right call while the 2026 offseason reveals new values.

Escuelas — HOLD (build around 2026 draft)

Year 5 of the rebuild. The 2026 rookie draft picks are the most important assets on the roster. Don't trade them.

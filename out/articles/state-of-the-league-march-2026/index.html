State of the League: March 2026

March 15, 2026. The NFL Draft is six weeks away. The BMFFFL Rookie Draft is eleven weeks away. Here's where every owner stands.

Where We Are

We're in the longest window of dynasty uncertainty: post-free-agency-opening, pre-draft. NFL teams are reshaping their rosters in real time. Players are changing teams. Coaching staffs are being finalized. Target shares are unresolved.

For BMFFFL, this means:

  • 2025 season is complete. tdtd19844 won the championship. MLSchools12 went 13-1 and lost in the semis. Again.
  • Free agency opened March 12. Moves are happening that will change dynasty values.
  • The NFL Draft (April 24-26) will determine landing spots for the 2026 rookie class.
  • The BMFFFL Owners Meeting (May) sets the rules for 2026.
  • The Rookie Draft (June 5) is the main event of the dynasty offseason.

Everything from now until June 5 feeds into that rookie draft. That's where the dynasty leverage is.

Who Won 2025

Champion: tdtd19844 (THE Shameful Saggy sack), 8-6 regular season, 4th seed, 152.92 def. Tubes94 135.08 in the final.

tdtd19844's championship is the third time in six seasons that an 8-6 team has won the title. The dynasty arc: 3-11 in 2022, gradual build, peak in 2025. The window opened and they climbed through it.

Runner-up: Tubes94 (Whale Tails), in their first championship appearance. 2024 saw them go 11-3 as well. The pieces are young enough that 2026 could be another deep run.

The 13-1 heartbreak: MLSchools12 put together their second 13-1 season in three years. They lost in the semis to tdtd19844, who was 8-6. This is now four semifinal exits from the top seed in six seasons. The structural problem is clear: the #1 seed in BMFFFL draws the hottest team in the bracket. The teams that get in with 8-6 records are dangerous precisely because they've had something to prove.

Current Dynasty Landscape (March 2026)

Tier 1: Championship Contenders

MLSchools12 — Despite the 2025 semifinal exit, they enter 2026 as the favorites. Two championships. 68-15 career record. 100% playoff rate. They need one thing: to not be the top seed.

Tubes94 — Back-to-back playoff appearances including a championship run. The roster is young and well-constructed. This is one of the two teams most likely to win in 2026.

JuicyBussy — Consistently competitive. 5 playoff appearances in 6 seasons. Won it all in 2023. The 2026 offseason will determine if the window is still open.

Tier 2: Playoff Likely

tdtd19844 — Defending champion. Dynasty windows close fast (see: Grandes, Cogdeill11). But they won with an 8-6 team, which means the roster has depth rather than peak variance. They may sustain.

SexMachineAndyD — 2024 runner-up. 2024 was an 11-3 regular season. If the same roster is intact, this is a contender.

rbr — Two-time runner-up (2021, 2022), zero championships. One of the most efficient offseason managers in the league. Always competitive, never quite over the top.

Tier 3: Rebuild Needed

Grandes — 2022 champion, 4-10 in 2025 (Moodie Bowl). The dynasty arc closed hard. The window closed fast. Will need significant infusion from the 2026 rookie draft to restart.

Cogdeill11 — 2020 champion. Slow decline since then: 2024 was 4-10. Not in rebuilding by choice, but by circumstance. The 2026 offseason is a make-or-break point.

eldridsm — 2020 runner-up. Peak seasons were 2020 and 2023 (9-5 each). 2024-2025 were 4-10 and 5-9. The roster needs an inflection.

Cmaleski — Never above 9-5 in any season, two playoff appearances in six years. Steady but not explosive. Needs the right buy-low window.

eldridm20 — 2023 runner-up (first championship appearance). 2024-2025: 6-8 each year. Inconsistent but occasionally dangerous.

Tubes94 — Jumping tiers in 2024-2025. The upward trajectory is steep and may continue.

Escuelas — 0/4 playoff appearances since joining in 2022. 6-8 in 2025 was a career high. Significant roster work needed.

The 2026 Rookie Draft Class

The 2026 NFL Draft class is potentially elite at the top — deep WR group, quarterback class that will be debated. Full dynasty analysis in the BMFFFL corpus.

Key names to watch:

  • The top WR prospects (class is WR-deep — historically the best dynasty class type)
  • Landing spots will be decisive — a WR1 going to a pass-heavy offense is worth more than the same WR going to a run-first team
  • QB class — in a Superflex league, rookie QBs with clean landing spots carry significant upside

BMFFFL teams with high rookie draft capital: Several teams with bottom-half records in 2025 will have top-3 picks. The rebuild teams (Grandes, Escuelas, Cogdeill11) will have early selections.

The Rookie Draft order is based on 2025 reverse standings. Final order confirmed at the Owners Meeting.

What Every Owner Should Be Doing Right Now

If you're a contender: Target win-now pieces. Free agency moves are changing team contexts — identify which of your players just got their target share improved or diminished.

If you're rebuilding: Watch the NFL Draft. Your 2026 rookie picks become significantly more valuable once landing spots are known. Don't sell them before April 26.

Everyone: Map your roster's age distribution. The average age of dynasty contributors tells you how many seasons of competitive window you have. Teams that peaked in 2021-2022 are aging out. Teams that built through 2023-2024 rookies are entering their prime.

Annual Awards

The 2025 BMFFFL
Awards Ceremony

Recognizing excellence, suffering, resilience, and audacity across the 2025 BMFFFL season. Your Commissioner, Bimfle, presiding.

The Winners Circle

Award 1

Most Valuable Manager

Winner

mlschools12

"Just doing what I do."

Four championships across all eras (2016, 2019, 2021, 2024). 13-1 regular season record in 2025 (and again in 2023) — the only manager to ever achieve that mark, and did it twice. The standard everyone else is chasing.

Award 2

Best Trade of the Year

Winner

rbr

"I just saw the value. Had to move."

Orchestrated the off-season's most praised deal — flipping a late-round pick and a declining veteran for a top-10 dynasty asset at a position of need. The league consensus: rbr won this trade by a wide margin.

Award 3

FAAB King

Winner

juicybussy

"I bid with my heart."

Led the league in total FAAB spend efficiency — multiple high-value adds at pivotal moments, including a $42 claim in week 9 that turned a middling week into a victory. The waiver wire is juicybussy's playground.

Award 4

Comeback Story

Winner

tubes94

"The hunt continues."

From 6-8 in 2023 to runner-up in 2025. tubes94 quietly assembled one of the best rosters in the league, peaked at 11-3, and made the championship game. The arc is not over.

Award 5

Best Draft

Winner

cmaleski

"Finally got the guys."

2025 rookie draft saw cmaleski execute a patient, needs-based strategy — landing a top-15 dynasty RB prospect in the 2nd round and a late-round TE dart that is already seeing target share. Grade: A.

Award 6

Worst Beat

Winner

tubes94

"The football gods are cruel."

Led the league in opponent points for during the 2025 regular season. tubes94 scored enough to win the championship game — except their opponent scored more. The cruelest irony: the best roster lost to the hottest peak.

Award 7

Dynasty Builder

Winner

tdtd19844

"The arc never ends."

The 2025 BMFFFL champion — won it the hard way as the 4-seed dark horse. Defeated Tubes94 152.92–135.08 in the title game. Built a durable, age-balanced roster over years of patient accumulation. The window wasn't opening — it was already open.

Award 8

Most Likely to Win 2026

Winner

tubes94

"The revenge arc is written."

Runner-up in 2025. Best roster going into 2026. Chips in the right spots. Every observer's consensus pick. Bimfle officially endorses this prediction while accepting no liability for playoff outcomes.

Office of the Commissioner

Bimfle’s Closing Statement

“Managers of BMFFFL — six seasons. Six champions. One dynasty.

mlschools12, what you have done to this league is both inspiring and clinically concerning. Four championships. A 13-1 season that ended in heartbreak. We are all, effectively, playing for second place, and yet: we return. Every year. Because the chase is the point.

tubes94, your revenge arc has been officially certified. The runner-up trophy is cold comfort, but 2026 is yours to lose. Do not lose it.

To escuelas — your commitment to fielding a roster in all six seasons, against all statistical evidence that things will improve, is the most human thing about this league. We salute you.

To everyone who traded, streamed, bid, cursed, and returned — this is what dynasty is. The arc is long. The arc bends toward a championship, eventually, for someone.

See you in 2026. ~Bimfle.”

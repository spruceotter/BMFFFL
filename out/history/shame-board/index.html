The Moodie Bowl

At the end of every BMFFFL season, while the glory-seekers compete in the championship bracket, two managers fight a different battle: the Moodie Bowl — the consolation bracket final. The winner earns the dubious honor of "not last." The loser earns last place, the worst draft pick, and full Shame Board immortality. It is contested with real emotion, because nobody wants to be the one staring up from the bottom.

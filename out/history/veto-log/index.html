Veto Policy

BMFFFL operates under a no-veto policy. Trades are not vetoed for value imbalance — only for demonstrated bad-faith collusion. Managers are trusted to make their own decisions, good or bad. In 6 seasons, no veto has been required.

Appeal Process

Any owner who believes a trade ruling was improperly decided may contest the decision through the following process:

  1. Message the commissioner within 48 hours of the ruling. Appeals submitted after the window are not eligible.
  2. State your grounds clearly. "I disagree with the outcome" is not grounds for appeal. Evidence of procedural error or undisclosed conflicts of interest may be.
  3. Majority vote of remaining owners decides the appeal if the commissioner is recused or if four or more owners formally contest the ruling. The commissioner does not vote in their own appeal.

Note: No appeal has ever been formally filed in BMFFFL history.

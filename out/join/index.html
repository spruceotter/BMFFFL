Why BMFFFL?

What makes this league worth your time

10 Seasons of History

Founded 2016. Six unique champions. A dynasty-format league with real stakes, real trades, and real history that compounds year over year.

12 Serious Managers

Every spot in BMFFFL is occupied by someone who actually watches football. No autopilot teams, no ghosts — the competition is real every week.

Active Commissioner

The Commissioner runs a tight ship: weekly engagement, annual rule reviews, trade oversight, and a growing digital archive of the league's history.

Dynasty Format

Keep your players. Build your roster over years. A 2023 rookie you drafted can still be starting for you in 2028. Dynasty is the purest form of the game.

Sleeper Platform

The best dynasty platform available. Live scoring, deep trade tools, rookie rankings, and a mobile-first experience that makes managing your roster enjoyable.

Real Prize Pool

Cash buy-in, cash payout. Champion, runner-up, and regular-season title all pay out. The money is secondary to the competition — but it is very much real.

What We're Looking For

✓Watches NFL games regularly — not just your fantasy players
✓Can make the annual buy-in without hesitation
✓Sets their lineup every week — no excuses, no ghosting
✓Engages with trade offers in 48 hours or less
✓Understands (or is willing to learn) dynasty roster strategy
✓Shows up for the June rookie draft — live, on time
✕Expects to win their first season — this isn't redraft

How It Works

The owner journey, start to ring

01
Get on the waitlist
Reach out to the Commissioner. If your application is strong, you go on the list.
02
Spot opens
When an owner steps away, the Commissioner evaluates the waitlist and offers the roster.
03
Inherit or rebuild
You receive the departing owner's roster — full of picks, players, and history. Build from there.
04
June Rookie Draft
The first test. Build your future through the annual rookie draft. Every pick matters.
05
Compete
September through January — the season is real, the scoring is live, and the competition is serious.

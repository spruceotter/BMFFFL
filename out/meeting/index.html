Current League Rules

Reference during the meeting. Click any rule to expand.

Roster Format & Scoring

Lineup: QB · RB · RB · WR · WR · WR · TE · FLEX · FLEX · SUPER_FLEX

Bench: 16 spots (cut to 12 before season)

Taxi squad: 5 players

IR: Off offseason/preseason; may turn on in-season

Scoring: Full PPR (1.0 rec) · 4-pt passing TDs

Trades

Counter window: 24 hours after a trade is proposed

No-counter signal: Both sides include $1 FAAB → counter window waived, trade processes immediately

FAAB — Prop D(OGE) System

Rate: 5 FAAB = 1 Dogecoin (current; has increased 3× since original)

Treasury: All Doge held in escrow by Commissioner

Annual refresh: Owners buy additional FAAB by paying Doge at market price; pool total = prior year's total FAAB spend

In-season sell windows: Weeks 4, 8, 12, 16

Mid-season buy: After Week 8, before Week 11 — bottom 6 teams only, up to $300 FAAB

Cash-out (offseason): Up to $500 FAAB in $100 increments at variable sell rate

Playoffs

Format: 6-team bracket

Auto bids: 3 division winners (regardless of record)

Wild cards: 3 remaining spots by record (points tiebreaker)

Byes: Seeds 1 & 2 — Seeds 3–6 play Round 1

Preseason Cut-Down (2026+)

Blocker players: 2 added to each of the 6 prior-year playoff teams when FA opens (late June/July) — occupy bench slots until cut

All-team cut: All 12 teams cut 4 bench spots (16 → 12) before the season

Playoff teams drop their blockers once the season begins as normal roster management

Dispersal Draft (2026)

Trigger: Escuelas left the league — new owner needed

Format: Snake draft, randomized order — 3–4 teams participate

Pool: All participating teams contribute full roster + 2+ years of picks anonymously; new owner builds squad from pool

Bonus: One late 1st this year + one next year added to pool

Open governance questions on FAAB disposition pending meeting vote

Platform

Why Sleeper?

BMFFFL has run on Sleeper since Season 1. It's not just where the league lives — it's genuinely the best dynasty fantasy football platform on the market. Here's why.

Instant Push Notifications

Trade offers, injury alerts, waiver results, and lineup reminders delivered in real time. No more checking in manually — Sleeper brings the league to you.

League Chat Built In

Every transaction auto-posts to the league chat. No third-party apps, no Discord setup required — the banter lives where the league lives.

Live Scoring

Watch your team's score update play by play during NFL games. Sleeper's live scoring is fast, accurate, and keeps the Sunday experience intense.

Trade Hub

Send, counter, and negotiate trades directly in the app. Sleeper's trade calculator gives you a sanity check before you send that regrettable offer.

Commissioner Tools

Penalty management, veto votes, roster overrides, and formal commissioner posts — everything the Commish needs to run a tight ship.

Dynasty Support

Full support for taxi squads, devy rosters, keeper rules, and rookie drafts. Sleeper was built for dynasty leagues, not bolted on as an afterthought.

Official Reference

League Communication
& Sleeper Guide

The authoritative BMFFFL reference for how the league uses Sleeper — notification settings, official communication channels, transaction announcements, and the Commissioner's protocols for league business.

Communication

Official Communication Channels

League Chat

The primary forum for all league business — general discussion, trash talk, announcements, and reactions to transactions. All official posts appear here.

Commissioner Posts

Formal announcements from the Commissioner covering rule changes, meeting notices, and binding league decisions. These are pinned and require acknowledgment.

Trade Announcements

All completed trades are posted to league chat automatically by Sleeper, accompanied by a Commissioner note when applicable.

Waivers & FAAB Results

Waiver processing results and FAAB bid outcomes are delivered as automated Sleeper notifications and posted to league chat.

How to Configure

Open Sleeper → tap your avatar (top-left) → Settings → Notifications. Toggle each category independently. Push notifications must be enabled at the device OS level for alerts to fire outside the app.

Transactions

Transaction Announcements

Trade Completed

Sleeper auto-posts the trade summary to league chat the moment it clears. The Commissioner may append a note for context or commissioner review outcomes.

FAAB Bid Results

Results are processed and posted Monday or Tuesday morning, depending on the weekly schedule. Each owner receives a push notification confirming their awards.

Waiver Order Changes

After each waiver run the updated waiver priority order is reflected in the app. Changes are announced in league chat automatically.

Free Agent Adds & Drops

Every add and drop outside of FAAB is posted to league chat in real time, including the player name, acquiring team, and dropped player.

Bimfle's Official Communication Protocols

“It is my most solemn and considered opinion — informed by the accumulated weight of league history, constitutional precedent, and the Commissioner's own expressed wishes — that all matters of league governance must be conducted through the appropriate and sanctioned channels of the Sleeper platform. One must never conduct league business via direct message alone. Transparency is the foundation upon which a great league endures.”

— Bimfle, AI Commissioner Assistant, BMFFFL

Governance Rules

  • All rule changes must be announced via a formal Commissioner post in league chat, followed by a 24-hour acknowledgment window during which owners may raise questions or objections.

  • Trade disputes must be raised within 48 hours of the transaction appearing in league chat. Disputes submitted after this window shall not be considered.

  • The Owners Meeting agenda shall be posted to league chat no fewer than seven days in advance of the meeting date.

  • One must never conduct league business via direct message alone. All binding communications must appear in the official league chat.

Decorum & Conduct

  • Commissioner posts shall be treated with the gravity appropriate to official league correspondence. Owners are expected to read and acknowledge them in a timely manner.

  • Trash talk, while celebrated as a proud tradition of the BMFFFL, shall never be permitted to obscure or bury an active Commissioner post. Commissioners may re-pin as necessary.

  • Any owner failing to set a legal lineup without prior notification to the Commissioner shall be considered to have forfeited the right to appeal that week's result.

  • Draft room etiquette requires that all owners be present at the announced start time. Late arrivals are managed via Sleeper's auto-draft function without recourse.

League Rules

Official rules reference for the BMFFFL dynasty league. Commissioner has final say on all disputes.

General Rules

Roster, FAAB, trades, and disputes

Format
12-team dynasty league. Superflex (2 QB/SF spots). Full PPR scoring.
Roster Size
10 starters + 12 bench spots + 5 taxi squad spots + 3 IR spots = 30 total.
Starting Lineup
1 QB, 2 RB, 3 WR, 1 TE, 2 Flex (RB/WR/TE), 1 Superflex (QB/RB/WR/TE) — 10 starters total.
Taxi Squad
5 spots for players in their first 2 seasons of NFL eligibility. Must declare taxi squad players before Week 1 each season.
IR Spots
3 IR/Reserve spots. Player must have an official IR designation to be eligible.
QB Limit
Maximum 4 quarterbacks on active roster during the regular season. IR and taxi squad slots do not count toward this limit.
FAAB
DogeFAAB — dynamic Dogecoin-backed blind bid budget. Refreshes each offseason. Minimum bid is $0.
Waiver Priority
After FAAB blind bid, priority resets each week. The last team to successfully claim moves to last priority.
Trade Deadline
After Week 14. Trades including future draft picks are allowed year-round outside of the deadline restriction window.
Scoring Disputes
Commissioner has final say on all scoring disputes. Disputes must be raised within 48 hours of the score being posted.

Playoff Rules

6-team bracket, Weeks 15–17

Playoff Field
6 teams qualify — seeds 1 through 6 based on regular season standings.
Playoff Schedule
Weeks 15, 16, and 17. Three rounds total.
First-Round Bye
Top 2 seeds receive a bye in Week 15 (first round). Seeds 3–6 play in the first round.
Bracket
Standard single-elimination bracket seeded 1–6. Higher seed is home team (no home-field advantage in scoring).

Rookie Draft Rules

Linear format, 4 rounds

Rookie Draft Format
Linear format, 4 rounds. Pick order is the same in every round (snake rules do NOT apply).
Rookie Draft Timing
First Friday of June (est. Jun 6, 2026 for the 2026 season).
Draft Order — Non-Playoff Teams
Picks 1–6 assigned to non-playoff teams in reverse order of final regular season standings (worst record picks first).
Draft Order — Playoff Teams
Picks 7–12 assigned to playoff teams in order of playoff seeding (lowest seed picks first among playoff teams).
Future Pick Trading
Future draft picks (up to 2 years out) may be included in trades year-round.
